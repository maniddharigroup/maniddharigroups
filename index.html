Maniddhari Gems

Maniddhari Gems stands as the jewel of our group, renowned for its expertise in crafting luxury, timeless jewelry. Our creations beautifully blend age-old traditions with modern design sensibilities, offering everything from regal bridal sets and temple jewelry to elegant, contemporary pieces for everyday wear. Every ornament is meticulously designed by our artisans, ensuring unmatched craftsmanship, purity, and elegance. We also undertake exclusive bespoke commissions for clients from across India and internationally.

Catering Services

Maniddhari Catering is synonymous with gourmet vegetarian excellence. Our menu showcases a rich tapestry of flavors, featuring everything from authentic Indian delicacies to globally inspired dishes, with a focus on Jain-friendly preparations. Whether it's an intimate gathering or a grand wedding banquet, our expert chefs curate each menu with creativity and precision. From live counters to customized desserts and signature beverages — every detail is planned to impress and satisfy the most discerning guests.

Event Planning

Our event planning service transforms celebrations into unforgettable experiences. With a dedicated team of planners, stylists, and coordinators, Maniddhari Group ensures that each event is executed with seamless precision and flair, leaving lasting impressions on your guests.
